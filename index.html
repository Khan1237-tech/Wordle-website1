💡 Pro Tips for Wordle Success

1

Start with Vowel-Rich Words

Begin with words containing multiple vowels like "AUDIO" or "RAISE" to quickly identify vowel positions.

2

Use Common Consonants

Include frequent consonants like R, S, T, L, and N in your early guesses to eliminate possibilities.

3

Think Strategically

Each guess should reveal new information. Avoid repeating letters you've already confirmed don't exist.
